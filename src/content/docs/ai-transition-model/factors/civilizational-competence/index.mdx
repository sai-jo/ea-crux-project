## Overview

Civilizational Competence refers to humanity's collective ability to navigate the AI transition successfully. This encompasses governance capacity, epistemic health, and societal adaptability.

## Relationship to Scenarios

Civilizational Competence acts as a **protective factor** - higher competence decreases risk across multiple scenarios:

- **AI Takeover**: Better governance and oversight reduces probability
- **Human-Caused Catastrophe**: Better coordination prevents misuse escalation
- **Long-term Lock-in**: Shapes whether lock-in is beneficial or harmful

Racing intensity measures the competitive pressure that drives AI developers to prioritize speed over safety. Even when actors prefer safe outcomes, the logic of competition creates a multipolar trap where rational individual behavior produces collectively dangerous results. Investing in safety while competitors cut corners means falling behindâ€”and falling behind feels existential to labs and nations alike.

Evidence shows intensifying dynamics: ChatGPT's 2022 launch triggered industry-wide acceleration, compressing safety evaluation timelines by 40-60%. Release cycles shortened from 18-24 months to 3-6 months. Safety budgets declined from 12% to 6% of R&D spending. The Racing Dynamics Impact Model quantifies these effects: racing reduces safety investment by 30-60% and increases alignment failure probability by 2-5x.

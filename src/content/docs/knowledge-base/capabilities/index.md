This section catalogs significant AI capabilities, their current state, and safety implications.

## Categories

### Foundational Capabilities
- [Large Language Models](/knowledge-base/capabilities/language-models) - Foundation models with emergent abilities
- [Reasoning and Planning](/knowledge-base/capabilities/reasoning) - Chain-of-thought and o1-style reasoning
- [Situational Awareness](/knowledge-base/capabilities/situational-awareness) - AI understanding its own context

### Agency & Autonomy
- [Agentic AI](/knowledge-base/capabilities/agentic-ai) - Systems that take actions in the world
- [Tool Use and Computer Use](/knowledge-base/capabilities/tool-use) - Calling APIs, browsing, executing code, controlling computers
- [Long-Horizon Autonomous Tasks](/knowledge-base/capabilities/long-horizon) - Multi-day/week autonomous work

### Advanced Capabilities
- [Autonomous Coding](/knowledge-base/capabilities/coding) - AI writing, debugging, and deploying code
- [Scientific Research](/knowledge-base/capabilities/scientific-research) - AI conducting research and making discoveries
- [Self-Improvement](/knowledge-base/capabilities/self-improvement) - AI improving AI systems
- [Persuasion and Social Manipulation](/knowledge-base/capabilities/persuasion) - AI's ability to influence humans

### Safety-Relevant Capabilities
- Honesty and calibration
- Following instructions precisely
- Explaining reasoning (interpretability)

## How to Read Capability Profiles

Each capability profile includes:
- **Current Level**: Where are we now?
- **Projected Timeline**: When might this become transformative?
- **Safety Implications**: What risks does this capability enable or mitigate?
- **Related Labs**: Who is working on this?

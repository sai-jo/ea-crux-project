Demis Hassabis is Co-founder and CEO of [Google DeepMind](/knowledge-base/organizations/labs/deepmind/), one of the world's leading AI research laboratories. A chess prodigy and video game designer turned AI researcher, he has led major breakthroughs including AlphaGo, AlphaFold, and the Gemini model family.

Hassabis has become an increasingly vocal advocate for AI safety, warning about existential risks while continuing to push the boundaries of AI capabilities.

This section profiles the major organizations shaping AI development and safety research.

## Categories

### Frontier Labs
Companies training and deploying the most capable AI systems:
- [Anthropic](/labs/anthropic) - Claude, Constitutional AI
- [OpenAI](/labs/openai) - GPT series, RLHF pioneer
- [Google DeepMind](/labs/deepmind) - Gemini, AlphaFold

### Research Organizations
- [MIRI](/labs/miri) - Agent foundations, decision theory
- [ARC](/labs/arc) - Eliciting Latent Knowledge, capability evaluations
- [Redwood Research](/labs/redwood) - Interpretability, AI control
- [CAIS](/labs/cais) - Center for AI Safety, field-building

### Policy & Governance
- [GovAI](/labs/govai) - AI governance research, compute governance

### Academic Groups
- [CHAI](/labs/chai) - UC Berkeley, human-compatible AI

## How to Read Lab Profiles

Each lab profile includes:
- **InfoBox**: Quick facts (founded, location, funding, headcount)
- **Overview**: Mission and approach
- **Safety Research**: Specific safety work and publications
- **Key People**: Leadership and notable researchers
- **Related**: Links to relevant risks, agendas, and capabilities
